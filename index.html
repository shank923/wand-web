About Wand

Wand is a smart pen with temporary ink that sends to your smartphone every scratch you made on a piece of paper the way you wrote it. It also recognizes and converts your text to digital format. The temporary ink evaporates after about 30 minutes from the paper allowing you to see what you’ve written and reuse the same piece of paper aftewards while your data is stored nice and safe on your phone, and, if you want, backed up on our servers.
